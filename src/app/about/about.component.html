About Us - We will serve you better

OvitFleet Services (OS), is the fastest growing, full-service Ground Support Equipment (GSE) and truck repair facility. With a managing team of over 20 years’ experience, our certified technicians can customize solutions for your company’s goals.

​

Due to high demand for GSE fleet maintenance and technical support, we’ve striven to become the trusted partner our customers can depend on. Founded in 2015, we’re proud of many accomplishments

Recognized by The National Minority Supplier Development Council as the only GSE certified Minority Business Enterprise fleet maintenance company in the U.S. Our Services is granted full security clearance with permits to access on-site airfield service and towing of equipment.

We are a certified contractor for large Ground Support Equipment manufacturer assisting with warranty repairs as well as decommissioning and commissioning of all related equipment. Our client base includes established relationships with major corporations, manufacturers, and renowned airlines.

Long standing relationship with customers who have referred business to us. Fleet Services LLC, has expanded from a one-man mobile operation to an 20000 Sq. feet service facility, including an additional 25000 Sq. feet of secure gated parking and 3 service vehicles. Our dedicated service team has grown from one to more than a dozen dependable employees. Over the years we have traveled to multiple airports for onsite technical support and maintenance.

Our Field of Vision includes providing zero-time Ground Support Equipment refurbishment, supporting the green movement, and continuing to diversify our fleet services offered to municipalities, small businesses, and corporations.
